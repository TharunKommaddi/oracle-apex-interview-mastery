Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get access to the platform?
Visit our Request Access page and fill out the access request form. Our team manually reviews each request to ensure quality and security. You'll receive an email with your login credentials once approved.
How long does access approval take?
Access requests are typically reviewed and approved within 24-48 hours during business days. Requests submitted on weekends may take until the following Monday.
Is the platform completely free?
Yes! APEX Interview Mastery is completely free to use. We believe in making high-quality Oracle education accessible to everyone. The approval process is only for maintaining platform quality and security.
Can I suggest new questions or improvements?
Absolutely! We welcome community contributions. Use the "Feature Request" option in the contact form above or email us directly with your suggestions. We review all submissions and credit contributors when their content is added.
What if I forgot my login credentials?
Contact us using the form above with the subject "Access Request Support" and include your registered email address. We'll help you recover your access.
How often is new content added?
We regularly update the platform with new questions, improved explanations, and additional resources. Major content updates are released monthly, with smaller improvements happening weekly.

Need Help? We're Here!

Don't hesitate to reach out. Our team is committed to helping you succeed in your Oracle career journey.
